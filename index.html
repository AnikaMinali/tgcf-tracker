Merit Ledger · 功德录
A Heaven Official's Blessing–themed daily tracker built for your TikTok editing journey. Complete tasks and habits to earn Spiritual Power, rank up through the celestial court, and collect TGCF sticker cards as rewards.
今Today — Your Daily Home
The Today tab is your home base. It shows your current rank, SP total, and progress toward the next rank — plus your daily habits with a tap-to-complete button.

Your day streak counts how many days in a row you've done any task or habit. The Videos Done counter tracks all completed quests. Below your stats you'll see your last few completed actions and a rotating motivational quote.
The speech bubble below the character cycles through chapter quotes — tap it to advance. The characters themselves react when you complete things.
任Quests — Tasks & Habits
Quests are one-off tasks. Each has a difficulty tier that determines how much SP you earn when you complete it — tap the circle on the left to check it off:

Blessing Quick or easy. Writing captions, planning a hook, importing clips.
Trial Normal effort. Filming footage, cutting and sequencing an edit.
Calamity Hard or scary. Posting the video, tackling a big collab, a complex edit.

Daily Habits live below the quest list. Add a recurring habit (like "Edit & post today's video") and track its streak separately. Each habit completion also earns you +1 🦋 butterfly.
Completing a Calamity triggers a crimson petal storm and an extra chibi reaction — reserve the tier for things that actually feel daunting.
气Spiritual Power & Ranks
Every completed task or habit adds SP to your total. The glowing bar at the very top of the screen and the progress bar on Today both reflect your progress toward the next rank. There are 8 ranks to climb:

Rank-up triggers a full-screen ascension ceremony — petals, a golden shockwave, and the characters celebrating. You earned it.
蝶Collect — Silver Butterfly Gacha
Every task you check off and every habit you cultivate earns +1 🦋 Silver Butterfly (shown in the top-right corner). Head to the Collect tab to spend them on card draws.

Cards come in three rarities:

Common 62% — kanji symbol cards for TGCF characters & items.
Rare 28% — illustrated scene cards from the series.
Legendary 10% — full art duo cards of Xie Lian & Hua Cheng.

There are 74 cards total to collect. Duplicate pulls give you +5 bonus SP instead.
人Characters & Interactions
Xie Lian (white-robed, right side) is always present. Hua Cheng (red-haired, left) unlocks once you reach Spirit Cultivator rank (600 SP) — a reward for consistency.

The characters react live to what you do: complete a Calamity and they celebrate; complete a habit and they cheer; leave the app idle for 30 seconds and one of them enters an idle pose with a thought bubble. Click either character to poke them. Click the speech bubble to cycle dialogue.
✦Tips
— Move your mouse for silver butterfly and parallax effects.
— The SP value on each task is editable — set it higher for things that actually take effort.
— Done > perfect. A rough edit posted beats a perfect edit sitting in drafts.
— Progress is saved automatically in your browser. Nothing is lost on refresh.
— Press Escape or click outside to close any overlay.
焚Reset the Ledger
Wipe all progress and begin the cultivation journey anew — your SP, rank, streak, quests, habits, journal, butterflies, and sticker collection will all return to their starting state. This cannot be undone.
Even I rebuilt the Puqi Shrine one brick at a time. If you must start over, start over without fear — the road remembers nothing but your next step.
